Dreams

During my service in GLZ radio I established the GLZ Podcasts Department. As part of my work as editor and producer I created the “Dreams” - “חלומות” podcast. Short radio stories episodes that reveal the realms we enter at night. I created 30 episodes that have been listened to over 50,000 times.

You’re welcome to listen to an episode in English, so you can get a glimpse of the project (Headphones are advised).

For each episode I also designed an individual banner to catch the visual essence of the dream.

A Croc Under the Bed

A nightly battle between a sleepy boy and the crocodile under his bed.

A Lion In The Alley

The israeli poet Rony Somek shared a childhood memory of a beloved alley that came to life in his dream.

A Soldier's Decision

As a parting gesture from GLZ radio I recorded my own dream into an episode. In the dream I am a heroic soldier who is called to save the day by slaying the mighty evil whale that's attacking Tel Aviv shores. I return victorious but quickly realise I've been fed false information and have been used as a marionette.
