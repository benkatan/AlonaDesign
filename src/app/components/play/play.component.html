Black Rain - Stage Design

In high school I majored in Theater studies and was assigned the role of stage designer in my senior year. I designed the set for the Israeli play “Black Rain” by Shimon Buzaglo. The play deals with the theme of the atomic bomb in past, present and future. The play was influenced by the British movie “When the Wind Blows”. Here you can see the conceptual art, sketches and images from the performance. I also took the liberty of designing the costumes if it would have been my part.
